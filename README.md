## ğŸ“Œ Project Summary

Hoobake is an innovative AI-powered platform designed to **connect customers with local bakers** tailored to their specific needs without the usual friction of using social media, direct messaging, or other scattered communication channels. Through a single user-friendly interface, users can **browse local bakers**, **describe their custom baked goods requirements**, **chat directly with bakers**, and **place orders seamlessly**. The platform also supports bakers by increasing their visibility, facilitating targeted customer engagement, and building community credibility.

The mission of Hoobake is to **simplify and personalize the bakery ordering experience** for both customers and bakers by leveraging intelligent search and communication tools.
